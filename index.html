School's Background

LCBA, for 9 decades, has provided quality education to the youth and adults of Calamba City and beyond. Through the years, it has embraced the changes in the educational system and innovated accordingly. Inspired by the principles of Dr. Jose P. Rizal, it has transformed many lives and still continues up to the present.
