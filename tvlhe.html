About TVL-HE

The TVL–HE Strand is best suited for students who enjoy cooking, baking, and food preparation, and are interested in careers related to hospitality, tourism, or caregiving.

Main Goals

Equip students with livelihood and service skills.

Provide real-world training for employment or business.

Prepare learners for TESDA certifications.

Develop entrepreneurial skills in home-based and hospitality careers.

What Students Learn
  • Cookery & Baking — food prep, baking, kitchen safety & sanitation
  • Housekeeping & Hospitality — room maintenance, hotel procedures, customer service
  • Tourism & Events — tour guiding, travel operations, event planning
  • Caregiving & Health — basic caregiving, assisting patients, wellness support
  • Beauty & Wellness — haircare, skincare, personal grooming
  • Entrepreneurship — small business, marketing, managing enterprises
TESDA Certifications
  • Cookery NC II
  • Bread and Pastry Production NC II
  • Housekeeping NC II
  • Caregiving NC II
  • Food and Beverage Services NC II

These certifications increase employability locally and abroad.

Career Opportunities
  • Chef / Cook Assistant
  • Baker / Pastry Maker
  • Housekeeping Attendant
  • Hotel / Restaurant Staff
  • Caregiver
  • Tour Guide
  • Event Coordinator
  • Entrepreneur
Related College Courses
